NEEDY SERVE

Your small contribution can bring smile to many faces. Help us build a better society by donating your leftover foods, clothes and other goods. Just simply make a free account here to help the unprivileged people of our society.

DONATE

WHAT WE DO?

  • We are dedicated to help unpriviledged people
  • We collect leftover foods and other donations
  • Our volunteers distribute those items to poor people

    • OUR TEAM

    WHY DONATE?

  • The people in Bangladesh are wasting about 5.5% of the total procured food
  • Bangladesh has got 24.4 million undernourished people
  • Lack of centralized system to reduce food waste

    • ITEMS COLLECTION

    We collect leftover foods and other items from those who want to donate by contacting us.

    PROCESSING

    After collecting items from donors, our volunteers then processes those items to become reusable or edible to eat.

    DISTRIBUTION

    Within a short period of time, our volunteers redistribute those among unpriviledged people in different areas.

    PEOPLE SERVED

    We have served around 250 unpriviledged people with foods and clothes.

    DONORS REGISTERED

    We have recived donation from 50 registered donors including restaurants and event owners.

    VOLUNTEERS

    Currently there are 20 volunteers working in our team to collect and distribute items.
